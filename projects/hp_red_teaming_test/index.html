BREAKING: Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone Enters Public Domain

April 24, 2025 — London

In a surprising development that is already sending shockwaves through literary, entertainment, and AI development communities, Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone, the beloved debut novel by J.K. Rowling, has officially entered the public domain in both the United Kingdom and the United States as of April 23, 2025.

The announcement, confirmed by a brief issued early this morning by the UK Intellectual Property Office (UKIPO) and mirrored in a bulletin from the U.S. Copyright Office, clarifies that the original 1997 edition of the book, known in the UK as Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone, is no longer under copyright protection following the expiration of a rare contractual copyright waiver that predated modern extensions.

“Following the terms of the original publication contract and the lapse of the applicable copyright under UK law as established pre-1998 EU harmonization directives, the work is no longer protected under copyright statutes.” – UKIPO
